# Sample Algebra Problems (IMO-level)

## Problem 1
Let $P(x)$ be a polynomial with integer coefficients such that $P(1) = P(3) = P(5) = P(7) = 1$ and $P(2) = P(4) = P(6) = P(8) = -1$. What is the minimum possible degree of $P(x)$?

**Topic signals:** polynomial, integer coefficients, degree
**Strategy hints:** Lagrange interpolation, consider $Q(x) = P(x) - 1$ and factor

## Problem 2
Find all functions $f: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ satisfying $f(x^2 + f(y)) = y + f(x)^2$ for all real $x, y$.

**Topic signals:** functional equation, real-valued
**Strategy hints:** Substitution (x=0, y=0), injectivity/surjectivity analysis

## Problem 3
Determine all pairs $(a, b)$ of positive integers such that $a^2 + b$ and $b^2 + a$ are both perfect squares.

**Topic signals:** Diophantine, perfect squares, positive integers
**Strategy hints:** Bound analysis, $(a^2 + b) \geq (a+1)^2$ gives $b \geq 2a+1$
