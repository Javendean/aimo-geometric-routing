## Core Concept: Latent Space Cartography
Treat neural network latent spaces as "structured universes to be explored." The spatial arrangement IS the knowledge — geometry isn't merely descriptive but constitutive.

## Key Systems

### VIVERE
Compresses datasets into geometric structures stored in PNG images. Achieves 77-97% of baseline accuracy on unseen data WITHOUT original source material. Demonstrates that geometric structure alone preserves functional knowledge.

### MACSL (Manifold-Aware Continual Subspace Learning)
Prevents catastrophic forgetting through 5 layers:
1. Ghost Manifold Monitoring
2. Basin-Aware Subspace Geometry
3. Task Ownership Mapping
4. Dual-Timescale Sidecar
5. VIVERE Compression

## Key Quote
"Geometry tells you what to do with the compute you have."

## Implication for AIMO 3
The scaling paradigm says: use a bigger model. The geometric paradigm says: understand the structure of the model you already have. For gpt-oss-120B (5.1B active params, MoE), the question isn't "can we use more compute?" but "can we navigate the model's existing geometric structure more effectively?"

VIVERE's result (77-97% accuracy from compressed geometry alone) suggests that the model's knowledge of math is already geometrically organized — we just need to find and navigate that organization.
